About the Office

ስለ ቢሮው

Lideta Subcity Building Permit and Control Office oversees construction activities, ensures compliance with city standards, and promotes safe and sustainable building practices.

የልደታ ክ/ከተማ ሕንፃ ፍቃድና ቁጥጥር ቢሮ የህንፃ ስራዎችን በመቆጣጠር ከመስፈርቶች ጋር ተዛመዱ መሆናቸውን ያረጋግጣል።
